[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, Pincode]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Designation]
[Company/Organization Name]
[Company/Organization Address]
[City, State, Pincode]

Subject: Application for Sick Leave

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I am currently unwell and unable to come to work. Due to my health condition, I am requesting a sick leave from [start date] to [end date]. I am expecting to be out of the office for [number] days and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am able to return.

I have attached a doctor's note to this application, which confirms my illness and recommends rest and recovery. I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to complete all my tasks as soon as possible.

I would like to thank you for considering my application. If there's any additional information you need from me, please don't hesitate to ask. I can be reached at [Your Phone Number] or [Your Email Address] if anything urgent comes up while I am away.

Thank you for your understanding and support.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Enclosure: Doctor's Note

Note: Please modify the application according to your needs and company policies. Also, make sure to include the necessary details such as the duration of leave, doctor's note, and contact information.
